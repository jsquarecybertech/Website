Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Jeel Makwana, J Square Cyber Tech emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: cybersecurity should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical expertise. We recognized that many small businesses and individuals were vulnerable simply because they found cybersecurity too complex or intimidating.

Our mission is to demystify digital security, providing clear, actionable solutions that anyone can understand and implement. Based in India, we've grown from a one-person operation to a dedicated team of cybersecurity enthusiasts committed to making the digital world safer for all.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our cybersecurity services

General Questions

What makes J Square Cyber Tech different from other cybersecurity firms?

We specialize in making cybersecurity accessible to beginners and non-technical users. While other firms might overwhelm you with technical jargon, we focus on clear, actionable solutions that anyone can understand and implement.

Do I really need cybersecurity services for my small business?

Absolutely. Small businesses are actually more vulnerable to cyber attacks than large corporations, as they often have valuable data but weaker defenses. Our services are specifically designed to be affordable and effective for businesses of your size.

How quickly can you help if I have a security emergency?

We prioritize emergency situations and can typically begin addressing critical issues within 24 hours of contact. For immediate assistance, call our emergency line at +91 7043333666.

Service Specifics

What exactly do you check during a Basic Security Audit?

Our audit covers: password policies, software updates, firewall configurations, backup procedures, physical security, employee access levels, and more. We provide a detailed report with simple explanations and prioritized recommendations.

How often should I run vulnerability scans?

We recommend monthly scans for most small businesses, with additional scans after any significant changes to your systems. Our scanning service includes scheduled automatic scans with easy-to-read reports.

What topics are covered in your Awareness Training?

Our training includes: recognizing phishing attempts, creating strong passwords, securing mobile devices, safe browsing habits, social engineering awareness, and reporting suspicious activity. We customize content based on your industry and specific risks.
